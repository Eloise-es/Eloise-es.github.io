English Teaching

I’m a CELTA-certified English teacher from Manchester, UK, and I taught English to adults in Paris for two years. I have also spent three years teaching French and Spanish in high school in the UK. I’ve studied French since I was 11 and started learning Spanish when I was 20, so I understand the ups and downs of the language learning process as both an adult and a child. I’ve found that the most important thing is to have fun with it, and I always encourage my students to try even when they’re not sure, as that’s how you improve! I'm currently teaching freelance and for Kickstart School in The Hague. Contact me if you are interested in learning English!
