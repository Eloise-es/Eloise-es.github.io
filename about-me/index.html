Web Developer Bootcamp

2022-present, Den Haag

I moved to the Netherlands in the summer and completed Colt Steele's Web Developer Bootcamp course from September 2022 to March 2023. I learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Node.js and built a full-stack Express app as the final project. I'm now continuing my studies and looking for entry level roles or traineeships in front-end development. I'm also teaching English part time, to adults and children, and learning Dutch!

Secondary School Teacher of French and Spanish

2019-2022, Manchester

I moved back to Manchester to do my teacher training, and went on to teach French and Spanish to students aged 11-18 in a school in Manchester. When coronavirus hit and schools had to close, I developed a passion for education technology. My years as a secondary school teacher were very intense - I learnt a lot, but I also burnt out from stress and working 60 hours per week.

Teacher of English as a Foreign Language

2017-2019, Paris

I trained to teach English as a foreign language (CELTA) and then moved to Paris, where I taught English to adults in a private language school. I really enjoyed the job, met lots of interesting people and improved my French, but it was never meant to be a long term thing, so in July 2019 I moved back to the UK to train to become a secondary school teacher.

BA Hons French and Linguistics

2013-2017, University of Leeds

My course suited me well because it was scientific as well as creative. I enjoyed learning about how languages work, and was lucky enough to have really passionate professors teaching me. As part of the French course, I spent a year teaching English in Normandy, France, which was an amazing first experience living abroad. I lived with Spanish speakers, which is how I ended up learning Spanish.
