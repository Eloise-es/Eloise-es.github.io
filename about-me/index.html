Web Developer Bootcamp

2022-present, Den Haag

I moved to the Netherlands in the summer and started Colt Steele's Web Developer Bootcamp course on 1st September 2022. So far I have learned HTML, CSS and basic JavaScript and have built this website with Bootstrap. I expect to finish the course in November 2022 and start looking for a job as a junior developer.

Secondary School Teacher of French and Spanish

2019-2022, Manchester

I moved to Manchester to do my teacher training, and went on to teach French and Spanish to students aged 11-18 in a school in Manchester. While teaching online during the coronavirus lockdowns, I developed a passion for education technology. My years as a secondary school teacher were very intense - I learnt a lot, but I also burnt out from endless 60 hour work weeks.

Teacher of English as a Foreign Language

2017-2019, Paris

I trained to teach English as a foreign language and then moved to Paris, where I taught English to adults in a private language school. I enjoyed it but after a while I felt like I needed something more, so I decided to move back to the UK to train to become a secondary school teacher.

BA Hons French and Linguistics

2013-2017, University of Leeds

My course suited me well because it was scientific as well as creative. I loved learning about how languages work, and was lucky enough to have really passionate professors teaching me. As part of the French course, I spent a year teaching English in Normandy, France, which was an amazing first experience living abroad and made me excited to explore more of the world.
