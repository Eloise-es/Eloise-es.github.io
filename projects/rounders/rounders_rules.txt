# ROUNDERS RULES

## 🎯 BEFORE YOU START - AGREE ON:
1. **Innings format** (choose one):
   - All batters bat once (shortest)
   - 27 good balls
   - 3 outs
   - Whole team out (longest)

2. **Good balls per batter**: Usually 1, but can be 2-3 (applies to everyone)

---

## 👥 TEAMS & PLAYERS
- **Ideal team size**: 6-15 players per team
- **Max on field**: 9 players at once
- **Positions required**: 1 bowler, 1 backstop, rest on field/covering bases
- **Game format**: Usually 2 innings

---

## 🏃 WHEN YOU MUST RUN:
- ✅ **If you HIT the ball** → MUST run (even on no-ball)
- ✅ **If you MISS a good ball** → MUST run
- ❌ **If you MISS a no-ball** → DON'T have to run (but you can)

---

## 🎯 SCORING:
### 1 Rounder:
- Hit ball and reach 4th post before next ball bowled

### ½ Rounder:
- Hit ball and reach 2nd or 3rd post (STOP there!)
- Miss ball but reach 4th post
- Bowler bowls 2 consecutive no-balls
- Fielder obstructs batter

### ⚠️ IMPORTANT:
- **Score only counts when you reach home safely!**
- If you get out during your run, you lose ALL points from that turn!
- If you stop at 2nd for ½ rounder, then continue and get out, you lose the ½ rounder!

---

## ⚾ BOWLING RULES:
### Must Bowl:
- Underarm with smooth action
- Between knee and head height
- Without bouncing
- Not too wide or at body
- Keep foot in square during bowling

### No-Ball Results:
- Batter doesn't have to run (if they didn't hit it)
- Can run and score normally if they want
- 2 consecutive no-balls = ½ rounder to batting team

---

## 🏃‍♂️ RUNNING RULES:
- Keep contact with post using hand, bat, OR foot
- Can run when ball leaves bowler's hand
- **Can't leave post when bowler has ball in square** (unless already running)
- If already running, can complete run to next post
- Must run outside batting square

### Backwards Hits:
- Can hit backwards and score!
- Must wait at 1st post until ball leaves backward area
- Then run normally

---

## ❌ WAYS TO GET OUT:
1. Ball caught before it bounces
2. Post you're running to is stumped
3. Lose contact with post when bowler has ball in square
4. Run inside batting square
5. Overtake teammate (THEY get out, you ran them out!)
6. Two batters at same post (first one there is out)

**Remember**: If out, you lose all points from that turn!

---

## 👥 MULTIPLE RUNNERS:
- Can wait at posts between batters
- Can run when next ball leaves bowler's hand
- Stay out there until you reach home or get out
- Can't have two at same post (first there is out)
- Can't overtake (person overtaken is out)

---

## 🏆 WINNING:
- Team with most rounders after 2 innings wins
- Innings ends based on agreed format

---

## 📋 QUICK REFERENCE:

| Situation | Result |
|-----------|--------|
| Hit ball + reach 4th | 1 rounder |
| Hit ball + reach 2nd/3rd (stop) | ½ rounder |
| Miss ball + reach 4th | ½ rounder |
| 2 consecutive no-balls | ½ rounder (batting team) |
| Fielder obstructs | ½ rounder (batting team) |
| Ball caught | OUT (lose points) |
| Post stumped ahead | OUT (lose points) |
| Overtake teammate | Teammate OUT |
| Two at post | First one OUT |
